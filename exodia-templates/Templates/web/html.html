About Exodia OS

Exodia OS is a highly customized Arch-based Linux distribution built for and by cybersecurity experts, as well as daily usage. It is capable of performing the most rigorous penetration tests and handling everyday tasks. Even if you're not a cybersecurity enthusiast, you'll appreciate Exodia OS for its aesthetically pleasing interface, tools, and features. Overall, Exodia OS is an excellent choice for programming and development, operations security, OSINT, and daily usage.
